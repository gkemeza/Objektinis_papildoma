Vilnius University is one of the oldest universities in Eastern Europe.
Its official website can be found at https://www.vu.lt/ and the short
version also works: https://vu.lt/. The Faculty of Mathematics and
Informatics has its own page at https://www.mif.vu.lt/en/.

For those interested in open source development, GitHub remains the
largest platform. Visit https://github.com/ or check out specific
projects like the Linux kernel at https://github.com/torvalds/linux.

Some links are written in markdown format, such as
[Vilnius University](https://www.vu.lt) or the faculty page
[MIF](https://www.mif.vu.lt/en/). Shortened bare links also appear
in text, for example www.vu.lt or www.github.com without a protocol.

European institutions maintain portals like https://europa.eu/ and
https://www.iana.org/. Stack Overflow at https://stackoverflow.com/
is frequently used by developers. Wikipedia is at https://www.wikipedia.org/.

Markdown style: [vu.lt](http://vu.lt/) and [GitHub](https://github.com).
